Change Purchase TAX

Easy change of taxes on purchase order form.

Why Change Purchase TAX?

Let me first give you an overview of this module.
This module will allow you to change taxes at one go for all your products listed in that purchase order form. It is a tedious task to manually change the taxes applied to each product listed in the product lines of purchase order. Keeping that in mind, we have brought a solution to this time consuming task.

Generally, we apply same kind of taxes to similar type of products that is to be purchased. To apply same tax to all the products of the order, Change Purchase TAX module of ours is best fitted. Our app allows you to apply multiple taxes too.

So, it saves your precious time that can be utilized to enhance your business instead. You might have not thought of, but this minute feature is of great significance.

To begin with, have the Change Purchase TAX Module downloaded in your system. For its proper setup, go through the below steps.

Change Purchase TAX Module
Firstly, you need to install the Change Purchase Tax App from Odoo Apps. It's easy to locate, just search on the name, you can identify it with the change tax icon. Ta-da, you are ready to go!!

Apps >> Change Purchase TAX >> Install

Purchase Order
Go to purchase app, open any RFQ order that contains list of products with different taxes.
To utilize this feature, tap on Action button. You will see Change TAX option in the drop-down list.

Apps >> Purchase >> Open any RFQ order >> Action >> Change TAX

Change TAX Wizard
Once you click on Change TAX, you will see a wizard. Fill it with the tax you want to apply to all the products listed in the product lines of that purchase order. After selecting taxes, to reflect the changes tap on Change Tax. You can also add multiple taxes.

Action >> Change TAX >> Add multiple taxes >> Change TAX

Changed Taxes
Now, have a look at the product lines of that order. You will be able to see that the taxes are changed. Isn't that so easy? Taxes can also be seen on the final amount.
