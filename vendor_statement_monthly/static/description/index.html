Monthly Vendor Statement

Access monthly vendor report for easy analysis

Why Monthly Vendor Statement?

Let me first give you an overview of this module.
This module will load all bills and refunds at one place and calculate the total credit and debit for a vendor every month. It is very tedious work to find all bills and refunds of a specific vendor of the required month from all the bills of various vendors and of various time periods. Not only that, once you find them, you have to manually calculate the total debit and credit for each vendor for each month.

In order to lessen your work, we have brought this app that will fetch all bills as well as refunds of a specific vendor and calculate the total debit and credit with the opening and closing balances for that month. This will save you a lot of time, which you can utilize to enhance your business instead.

There is one more perk of using this app. It allows you to download the monthly vendor statement in pdf form, so that you can easily share it with your vendors and save time explaining and analyzing monthly vendor balances.

Hot Features

Generates vendor's monthly statement

Detailed Features

  • Generate vendor's monthly statements with opening and closing balances.
  • One time scheduler will automatically generate monthly statements once at the time of its installment.
  • After which the daily scheduler will update monthly vendor statements once every day.
  • You can also manually create monthly statements for specific vendors of required month.
  • You only need to fill the month and vendor fields.
  • To load the bills and refunds, tap on Reload button.
  • All the bills and refunds of that particular vendor within the mentioned month will be displayed.
  • You can also download the monthly statement.
  • This will make it easier to share monthly statements with vendors.
To begin with, have the Vendor Statement Monthly downloaded in your system. For its proper setup, go through the below steps.

Vendor Statement Monthly Module
Firstly, you need to install the Vendor Statement Monthly App from Odoo Apps. It's easy to locate, just search on the name, you can identify it with the vendor statement icon. Hold on, you also need to know about the scheduler yet.

Apps >> Vendor Statement Monthly >> Install

Scheduled Action
The one time scheduler executed at the time of installment will automatically generate all monthly vendor statements till date.
Not to worry about new bills and refunds created after installing our module, we have a daily scheduler that can be activated from your general settings.

General settings >> Technical >> Scheduled Actions >> Vendor Statement: run scheduler daily Now you are ready to utilise this feature. For more information go through the Work Flow.

Monthly Vendor Statement Location
Open invoicing app, under reporting, you will find Monthly Vendor Statement.
Apps >> Invoicing >> Reporting >> Monthly Vendor Statement

You will find vendor statements already generated till date, isn't that amazing? Well, this is due to the one time scheduler executed at the time of its installment.

Create Form
You might think of manually creating a monthly statement of a specific vendor for a particular month. That is possible too!
Go to Monthly Vendor Statement and tap on create to get your customized monthly statement of a vendor for the required month.

Apps >> Invoicing >> Reporting >> Monthly Vendor Statement >> Create

Reload button action
Well, if you are stuck, here is a small guide on filling the form.
Under Month field select month and year as required and for Vendor field select your vendor. Do not bother about other fields.

Once these two fields are set, click on Reload button. All the bills and refunds of that particular vendor within the mentioned month will be displayed.

Download in pdf format
It's not over yet! There is one more thing you might want to know.
It also gives you a feature to download the monthly vendor statement in pdf form. This will make it very easy for you to share monthly vendor statements with your vendors.
To download the report, tap on Print option and select Monthly Vendor Statement.

Print >> Monthly Vendor Statement

Monthly vendor statement PDF
A pdf will be downloaded.
