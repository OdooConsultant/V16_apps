Why Inventory Stock Movement?

Let me first give you an overview of this module.
This module provides the ability to track all inventory movements for all warehouses and for all companies. Users can track inventory movements between specific dates and for specific location.

It's always painful for most of the business to track the inventory movements, especially when you have a large product catalogue or high frequent inventory transactions. This app helps you to track the inventory for specific periods for all products for all warehouse locations. Users can filter reports by product category, products, duration, warehouses and locations. Not to miss out, it also allows you to download the excel report.

So, it saves your precious time that can be utilized to enhance your business instead. You might have not thought of, but this minute feature is of great significance.

To begin with, have the Inventory Stock Movement Module downloaded in your system. For its proper setup, go through the below steps.

Inventory Stock Movement Module
Firstly, you need to install the Inventory Stock Movement App from Odoo Apps. It's easy to locate, just search on the name, you can identify it with the search inventory icon. Ta-da, you are ready to go!!

Apps >> Inventory Stock Movement >> Install

Inventory Stock Movement Location
Go to inventory app, under reporting, you will find Inventory Stock Movements.
Apps >> Inventory >> Reporting >> Inventory Stock Movements

Once you click on that, you will see a form view.

Form View
You will see the default data already present. It takes the default duration as current month. The mandatory fields are the duration, warehouse and location. You need to select the time period for which you want the records from the particular warehouse and location. You can also use the product category and product fields to filter your records.

Form >> Duration >> Warehouse >> Location

Refresh Button
If you have selected any product category or products it will only list out those products in the tree view. But if you haven't selected any of the two, it will list out all the products for the mentioned duration for that warehouse location. Click on the refresh button everytime you want the updated information.

Form >> Refresh

Not to miss out, you can see a zero movement boolean on the left. If you uncheck that it will only show products with movements.

List of Stock Movements
You will see the list of products with their category, opening stock, stock in, stock out and closing stock for the mentioned duration of that particular warehouse location. Isn't that amazing?

You can also see the Total Opening, Total In, Total Out and Total Closing at the top.

Product Stock Movements
You can checkout all movements for a particular product too. Click on any product in the tree view. A form view will pop up with product name, product category and product movements. Now, that's something you wouldn't have expected.

Form >> stock movement list >> click any product

Print Report Button
The most availing feature is still remaining. You can even download this as excel report. On clicking the save button, you would see two more buttons on the top i.e. Print and Action. Click the print button and select Inventory Stock Movement.

Form >> Save >> Print >> Inventory Stock Movement
